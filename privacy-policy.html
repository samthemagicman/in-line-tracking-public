Who We Are

In Line Tracking is a safety-focused group tracking app developed by In Line Apps. Our platform enables users to view the real-time location of group members, helping individuals stay connected and safe during their outdoor activities. The app is intended for public use and was built with community and safety in mind.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Real-time and historical location data
  • Payment information

Non-Personal Information

  • Device type and operating system
  • App usage statistics

How We Collect Information

  • Background location tracking (with user permission)
  • Information entered by users within the app

We do not use third-party login providers such as Google or Apple.

Purpose of Data Collection

  • Provide real-time location tracking and related features
  • Improve overall app functionality and user experience
  • Send important safety notifications
  • Conduct marketing activities (with an option to opt out)
  • Analyze usage data to enhance our services

We do not engage in automated decision-making or user profiling.

Data Sharing

We may share anonymized or aggregated data in specific circumstances:

  • With municipalities for infrastructure or public safety initiatives
  • With trusted third-party service providers for hosting and analytics under strict confidentiality agreements

We never sell your personal data.

Data Retention

Your data is retained only as long as necessary for operational or legal requirements. After that, it is securely deleted or anonymized to protect your privacy.

Your Privacy Rights

As a user, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct any inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of marketing communications

To exercise your rights, contact us at Inlineapps1.0@gmail.com. You can also manage your privacy settings in the app (some features may be limited during the Beta period).

Data Security

We are committed to protecting your information. Security measures include:

  • End-to-end encryption
  • Secure servers
  • Role-based access controls

In the event of a data breach, we will promptly inform affected users and the appropriate authorities.

Children’s Privacy

In Line Tracking is not intended for users under the age of 13. We use technical measures to prevent underage account creation. If we become aware of any data collected from a child under 13, it will be permanently deleted.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

While we do not use traditional cookies, we may use similar tracking technologies such as device identifiers. You can manage these settings through your mobile device’s privacy controls.

International Data Transfers

We currently do not transfer user data outside of Canada.

Legal Compliance

We are committed to complying with all relevant privacy laws and regulations, including:

  • Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA – Canada)
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR – EU)
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA – California, USA)
  • Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA – USA)

Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy as needed to reflect changes in our practices. In the event of significant updates, we will notify users via email.
